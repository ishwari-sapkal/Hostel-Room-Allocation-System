Hostel Fees Structure

Detailed fee information for all hostel residents

Total Annual Hostel Fee: ₹ 32,500
(Including Room Rent, Mess Charges & Maintenance)

📌 Hostel Fees Breakdown

Fee Type Details Amount (₹)
Room Rent Per year 12,000
Mess Charges Break/Lunch/Dinner (compulsory) 15,000
Maintenance Fee Electricity, cleaning, repair 3,500
Security Deposit Refundable at the end of the year 2,000

🏡 Facilities Included

  • Comfortable Bed & Cupboard
  • 24×7 Security & CCTV Surveillance
  • Solar Water Heater & RO Water
  • Study Room & Reading Hall
  • Daily Cleaning Services
  • Indoor/Outdoor Sports Facilities
  • Wi-Fi Access

💳 Payment Instructions

Students must pay the fees through any of the following modes:

  • Online Payment (UPI / Net Banking)
  • Bank Deposit (Receipt submission mandatory)
  • College Administrative Office
Note: Fees must be paid within 15 days after hostel allotment. Late fees will be applicable afterwards.
