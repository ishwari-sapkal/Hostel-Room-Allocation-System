Hostel Registration Instructions

Follow these steps carefully before applying for hostel admission

📝

Step 1: Fill Registration Form

Enter your full details correctly including name, mobile number, branch, year and address.

📄

Step 2: Upload Documents

Upload a clear Aadhaar card copy in JPG/PNG/PDF format while registering.

⌛

Step 3: Wait for Verification

Your details will be verified by the hostel admin. This may take up to 24 hours.

🏠

Step 4: Room Allocation

After verification, the admin will allocate a room based on availability.
