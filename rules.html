Hostel Rules & Policies

Your Safety • Your Comfort • Our Responsibility

General Rules

  • All students must carry their Hostel ID Card at all times.
  • Entry in the hostel is allowed only till 8:00 PM.
  • Visitors are allowed only in designated areas and timings.
  • Students must maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas.
  • Playing loud music or causing disturbance is strictly prohibited.

Room & Accommodation

  • Students are responsible for their personal belongings.
  • Furniture and hostel property must not be damaged or moved.
  • Electrical items like heaters, irons, and kettles are not allowed.
  • Rooms will be inspected regularly by hostel authorities.

Health & Safety Policies

  • Smoking, alcohol, or drug use inside campus is strictly prohibited.
  • Fire safety instructions must be followed at all times.
  • Report any illness or injury to the warden immediately.
  • Any suspicious activity should be informed to security staff.

Discipline & Conduct

  • Students must respect wardens, staff, and fellow residents.
  • Ragging or bullying is strictly prohibited and punishable.
  • Damage to property must be compensated by the student.
  • Leaving hostel after 8 PM requires written permission.

Leave & Out-Pass

  • Out-pass application must be submitted 24 hours before leaving.
  • Parents will be contacted for verification before approval.
  • Leaving hostel without permission is a serious violation.
