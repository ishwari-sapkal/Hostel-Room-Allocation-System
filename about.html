About Our Hostel

Our Legacy

Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel was established in 1958 in honor of Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh, a visionary leader, educationist, and former Union Minister for Agriculture. Dr. Deshmukh’s commitment to student welfare and education laid the foundation for this hostel, which has since nurtured thousands of students in a safe and supportive environment.

The hostel has been managed with guidance from his wife, Vimlabai Deshmukh, who dedicated herself to improving facilities and promoting a culture of discipline, learning, and holistic growth. Their vision continues to inspire the hostel community today.

Over the decades, the hostel has expanded its infrastructure, introduced modern facilities, and become a home away from home for students from across the region, keeping the legacy of its founders alive.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment for students to grow academically, socially, and morally, continuing the legacy of Panjabrao Deshmukh and Vimlabai Deshmukh.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading student hostel with excellent facilities, safety, and a culture of discipline and learning.
